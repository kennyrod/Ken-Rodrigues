UI Design

App UI Design (Android)

App screens and wireframes, based on the concept of making novels available to busy people in search of a moment of solitude.

Business Goal

The goal is to provide every kind of literature in a single hub, to a suitable age group.

Usability Goal

The usability goal is to make the user find their respective author or book without any hard work and all of it in one place.

User Persona

Deepika, a 30yr-old housewife, residing in Indore, searching for a medium to help her pass the time by exploring her hobby which is reading.
